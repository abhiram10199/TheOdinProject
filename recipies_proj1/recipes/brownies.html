Brownies, Fudgy Kind


A recipe to make fudgy brownies that I really like.

Ingredients

  • 100g all-purpose flour
  • 275g dark brown sugar
  • 100g chocolate chunks
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 200g unsalted butter
  • 100g white chocolate

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Melt the 200g chocolate and butter in the microwave
  3. Whisk together the eggs, salt, and brown sugar until pale and fluffy
  4. Add butter mix into the whipped egg mixture and gently stir
  5. Add the flour and cooca powder. Stir partially. Add chocolate chunks and then stir until fully combined
  6. Pour batter onto parchment lined tin and bake for 30m
  7. Cool and remove the tin. If desired, serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
